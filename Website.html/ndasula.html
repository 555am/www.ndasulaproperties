NDASULA PROPERTIES is a property development company which has decided to diversify into agriculture by pursuing a green scheme aimed at the production of vegetables and fruits for household consumption. The business will strive to become one of the Rundu leading producer of various vegetables for household consumption by promoting sustainable use and efficient management of natural resources. With a well-thought-out business plan executed by a skilled management team, Ndasula Green Scheme expects to generate over N$1.9 million and N$2.5 million in sales in year one and year two, respectively.
